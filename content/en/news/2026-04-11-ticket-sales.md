Hello! I'm n13u, chair of the organizing committee for Frontend & PHP Conference Hokkaido 2026. We're now less than two months out from the event…

This post announces that ticket sales and day-of staff recruitment are now open! As of 9:00 JST on April 11, 2026, attendee tickets are on sale via the official fortee event page!

## About ticket sales

First, a summary of ticket information. In addition to the regular attendee tier, this year we've added a separate tier for residents of Hokkaido. It's a discounted ticket for people who live in the prefecture (self-declared, but still…). The same number of tickets is allocated to each tier (Hokkaido residents and everyone else).

Sales open at 9:00 today, the day this article is published. The venue this year is larger than last year's, so there's plenty of room — please apply with confidence 🙇‍♂️

After-party tickets are limited in number and will be sold on a separate schedule. Joining the after-party requires a regular ticket, so if you're interested please grab one early.

We're also planning to offer separate free student tickets and discounted U25 tickets. Details are scheduled to be announced next week — please stay tuned if you're hoping to buy one.

The online ticket is free again this year, just like last year. It's prepared for those across Hokkaido and around Japan who can't make it on the day. Please consider it as well!

## Ticket overview

**Sales begin: April 11, 2026**

- General admission: ¥3,000 × 100
- Hokkaido resident tier: ¥2,500 × 100
- After-party ticket: ¥6,000 (planned)
- U25 tier: ¥1,500 × 10 (planned)
- Student tier: free × 10 (planned)
- Individual sponsor ticket: ¥5,000
- Online: free — sales page: fortee

\* Speakers and sponsors will receive separate ticket guidance, so please hold off on purchasing a general ticket.

Alongside ticket sales, Frontend & PHP Conference Hokkaido 2026 is also opening individual sponsor applications! Each unit is ¥5,000, and you may apply for as many units as you'd like!

## About day-of staff recruitment

We're also opening day-of staff recruitment starting today! We're looking for people who can help on the event day (June 6) and the surrounding days (June 5 and June 7). Day-of staff handle the basic day-of work — setup, teardown, reception, timekeeping, and so on. As mentioned, help with cleanup on the surrounding days would also be much appreciated!

If you join as day-of staff, we'll provide lunch on the day and a staff T-shirt. There's also no event participation fee. If you're interested in conference staffing, or if you love web frontend, PHP, or web technology in general, we'd love to hear from you.

### Recruitment overview

- Date: Saturday, June 6, 2026
- Main day: 09:00–19:00
- After-party: 19:00–21:00
- Place: Sapporo Convention Center
- Requirement: able to participate from the main day through the after-party on Sat. June 6
- Application period: Friday, April 10, 2026 – Thursday, April 30, 2026
